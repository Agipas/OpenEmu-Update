『BLADE BUSTER(ブレイドバスター)』
 
◆Story◆
 
Suddenly appearing from outer space, an airplane-like object of unknown
origin quickly reduced the plant and its garrison to smithereens.
 
Said the remaining soldiers, “it was as if they were on a fun hunting trip.”
Possessing overwhelming offensive power, amazing manouervability, and speed,
the blue airplane came to be called BLADE. 
 
Afterward, at a rate beyond our ability to plan for,
attacks by BLADE came one by one causing incredible damage.
To stop this, the plan to launch massive numbers of
unmanned drones, Blade Buster Project, was launched.


★Operating System★
 
 Requires an NES-supporting emulator.
 It is possible that it may run on a real system.
 Uses MMC3 CHR-ROM.

★User Guide★
 
　◆Title Screen
　　Up/Down/Select：Choose between 2min/5min
　　Start: Start game
 
　◆In Game
　　D-pad: Moves player
　　A/B: Shoot (Rapid fire)
　　Select: Speed select
　　Start: Not used
